# 📊 JoeUnTracker

**Mfumo wa Kisasa wa Kufuatilia Biashara - Made in Tanzania 🇹🇿**

---

## 🌟 Features

- 🔐 **User Authentication** - Jisajili na Ingia kwa usalama
- 📊 **Dashboard** - Muhtasari wa faida na hasara kwa siku, wiki, mwezi na mwaka
- 📦 **Product Management** - Ongeza, hariri na futa bidhaa zako
- 💰 **Sales Tracking** - Rekodi mauzo kwa kubonyeza mara moja tu
- 💸 **Expense Tracking** - Fuatilia matumizi yote ya biashara
- 📈 **Analytics** - Charts na takwimu za kina
- 📄 **Reports** - Pakua ripoti za PDF kwa vipindi tofauti
- 👥 **Business Network** - Ungana na wafanyabiashara wengine Tanzania
- 💬 **Chat System** - Wasiliana na wafanyabiashara wenzako
- 📱 **PWA Support** - Sakinisha kwenye simu kama app
- 🌙 **Modern UI** - Muonekano wa kisasa na responsive
- 🟢 **Real-time Notifications** - Arifa za faida na hasara
